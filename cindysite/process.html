My Work Process

 
 

Because I’m relatively new to coding I build sites just for fun. I sketch them out by hand and then mock them up in Illustrator. However, for class projects and for this site, I have gone through the exercise of conducting research and discovery, thinking about the site’s audience and users, pondering usability, and building wireframes and site maps.
