Coding, Creating, and Process
 
 

There is a certain process to almost any endeavor. In addition to learning to code, two endeavors that I’ve set my hand to in life are writing and creating art. When preparing to build this portfolio, I listed those things that I wanted to showcase—my coding prowess, my skills and accomplishments, my writing, and my artwork. As I was jotting all of this down it dawned on me that there is a certain commonality found in coding, writing, and creating art. This commonality boils down to process. Each activity includes the process of discovery and research, planning and defining goals, creating and refining, critiquing, further refining, and ultimately publishing or somehow making public.

In the coming months, I will be writing more about these common steps.
